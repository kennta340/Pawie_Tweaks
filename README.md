# Pawie Tweaks

A lightweight Quality of Life (QoL) addon for World of Warcraft 3.3.5a (WotLK / Ascension / Conquest of Azeroth). Created to remove daily annoyances, speed up gameplay, and make the world map much smoother – without draining unnecessary CPU or RAM.

## 🌟 Features

Most of these features can be toggled on and off in-game via `Esc > Interface > AddOns > Pawie Tweaks`.

### 🗺️ Map Tweaks
* **Movable Windowed Map:** The World Map is converted into a window that you can move around freely. 
* **Scroll-zoom:** Change the size of the map directly using your mouse scroll wheel, or drag the bottom right corner.
* **Fade on Move:** The map automatically becomes semi-transparent (50%) when you run, ride, or fly, and turns solid again when you stop.
* **Larger Player Arrow:** Your own position (the silver arrow) on the map is scaled up by 50% to make it easier to spot.
* **Clean UI:** Removes the clunky default borders and dropdown menus from the map.

### ⚡ Automation (No more clicking!)
* **Auto-Quest:** Accepts and turns in quests at lightning speed. 
  * *Smart Pauses:* The addon automatically pauses if it detects an Escort quest or an Event warning. You can also hold `SHIFT` while talking to an NPC to temporarily pause the feature.
* **Auto-Sell Junk:** Automatically sells all your grey junk items when talking to a vendor and prints how much gold you earned in your chat.
* **Auto-Confirm BoP:** Automatically clicks "Yes" on the warning prompt when looting "Bind on Pickup" items.
* **Skip "DELETE":** Automatically fills in the word "DELETE" in the confirmation box when trying to destroy rare or epic items.

### 🛠️ Interface & Fixes
* **Error Filter:** Hides and silences annoying red spam text on your screen (e.g., *"Not enough rage"*, *"Out of range"*, *"Nothing to attack"*).
* **Chat Copy Tool:** Adds a small, discreet button to the top right of your chat window. Click it to open a text box where you can easily copy links and chat history (`Ctrl+C`).

## 💬 Chat Commands
* `/pawie` - Opens a list of available commands.
* `/pawie fade` - Toggles the map transparency when moving on/off.
* `/pawie quest` - Toggles Auto-Quest on/off.
* `/rl` - A short and quick command to reload your user interface (Reload UI).

## 📥 Installation

1. Click the green **Code** button at the top right of this page and select **Download ZIP**.
2. Extract the downloaded ZIP file on your computer.
3. ⚠️ **IMPORTANT:** The extracted folder will likely be named `Pawie_Tweaks-main`. You **must** rename this folder to exactly `Pawie_Tweaks` (remove the "-main" part).
4. Move the `Pawie_Tweaks` folder into your WoW installation: 
   `Your-WoW-Folder\Interface\AddOns\`
5. Start the game and make sure the addon is enabled in your Addon list at the character selection screen. You're good to go!
